Our Story

About Meng Yong Seafood

Who We Are

Meng Yong Seafood is a premier aquaculture enterprise based in the nutrient-rich wetlands of Bagan Pasir, Perak. We specialize in the sustainable cultivation of premium mangrove oysters, bridging the gap between traditional farming wisdom and modern food safety science.

Our mission is simple: To put Malaysian oysters on the world map by delivering a product that is not only delicious but rigorously lab-tested and safe for raw consumption.

Our Heritage

Bagan Pasir, Perak is our home and our history. For over 30 years, this estuarine community has been the heart of the state's cockle farming industry. We are the next generation of that legacy.

By adapting deep local knowledge of tides and currents to modern oyster farming, we have turned these historic waters into a sanctuary for the "Premium BP Oyster"‚Äîa masterpiece of flavor and texture.

Single-Seed Origins

Raised from hatchery-raised single-seed spat. This modern technique ensures a perfect, deep-cup shape and consistent meat quality.

Raft & Cage Control

Farmed on floating rafts and kept in specialized cages. This method allows for better quality control and protection during growth.

Mangrove Nurtured

Grown in the biodiverse Bagan Pasir estuary. The mixing of fresh river water and saltwater creates a unique, creamy sweetness.

Science-Backed Safety

We combine tradition with innovation. Post-harvest UV depuration and rigorous periodic lab testing ensure every shell is pristine.

Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How do I store the oysters?

Keep them in the fridge (5-8¬∞C), cup side down, covered with a damp cloth to keep them moist. Do not store in water or an airtight container, as they need to breathe.

How long do they last?

For the best experience, we recommend consuming them within 3-5 days of delivery. They are live animals and stay fresh as long as they are kept cool and damp.

Are they safe to eat raw?

Yes. Our oysters are depurated (purified) using UV technology and regularly lab-tested for bacteria to meet strict food safety standards for raw consumption.

Where do you deliver?

We cover most major cities in West Malaysia via express next day delivery. Please WhatsApp us with your postcode to confirm coverage before ordering.
